Task Abstraction

High-level task: Produce: Annotate: This visualization adds on to information about daily temperatures by highlighting the temperatures of the last 3 days.

Medium-level task: Lookup: Since the last 3 days are identified, the target and location are both known.

Low-level task: Identify: The visualization identifies the data points for the last 3 days out of all points from 1979-2021.

Target: All Data: Outliers: The visualiztion shows how the last 3 days are outliers from the typical temperatures at that time of year.

High-level task: Produce: Record: This visualization takes snippets from daily mean temperatures and presents it as new information.

Medium-level task: Browse: The locations, Seattle and Portland, are known, but the target is unknown.

Low-level task: Summarize: The viewer should be looking at all data points.

Target: Attributes: One: Distribution: The visualization shows the distribution of points, which are concentrated on the right side, showing how temperatures have been abnormally high in recent years.
